# **Research Question**

## ¿How does wage vary with age in Bogota?

\vspace{0.5cm}
In Bogota, wages tend to increase with age until a mid-life point and then decrease.  

\vspace{0.5cm}
This is consistent with the **human capital theory**:  
- Wages increase at a decreasing rate.
